About Me

The year was 1986. He was a teenager like any other: dreaming of his heroes and in love with a girl.

But on a thunderous night along a ragged coast a mysterious wrecked car came to him. Its power lighting his eyes blood red.

In a flash, all was lost in a hellfire of twisted metal.

When our hero emerged from the burning wreckage, he and the car had become one - their souls spliced forever - leaving him to wander the night alone, invisible to everyone...but her.
